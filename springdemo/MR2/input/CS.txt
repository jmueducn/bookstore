Introduction to Algorithms:
A comprehensive introduction to the design and analysis of algorithms. This book covers a wide range of algorithmic techniques and provides insights into solving complex problems efficiently.

Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship:
Learn best practices for writing clean and maintainable code. This handbook, authored by Robert C. Martin, offers practical advice and examples to help developers improve their coding skills.

Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software:
Explore classic software design patterns and their applications. This influential book, commonly known as the "Gang of Four" book, provides essential insights into creating flexible and reusable software components.

Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach:
An in-depth exploration of artificial intelligence concepts and techniques. This comprehensive textbook, written by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig, covers a wide range of AI topics, including problem-solving, knowledge representation, and machine learning.
